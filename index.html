My interest in Technology and Coding

Since as long as I can remember I have always been passionate when it comes to Technology and only just in the past few years I have picked up a taste for coding. When I started secondary school I asked myself “How does my phone do what it does so easily?” and that is when I realised that softwares and electrical products such as phones and microwaves all needed coding to run. From here I tried to learn what I can about coding wherever I could.

What made me interested in CodeNation?

For years I wanted to learn about what coding was and how it worked but I had no clue where to start or who to ask. Till just a couple of months ago when a friend of mine completed the Develop: Cyber course at CodeNation in Manchester. He told me what CodeNation was about and what they specialise in, I then told him that I had zero experience with coding but he said it did not matter as most of the students that apply have no experience at all. I was shocked in the best way when I found out about this opportunity so I went home and did my research. I then found myself starting the Develop: Coding course in the Manchester branch. I am now just over a week into my course and I feel like I have learned what someone would have took maybe a year to learn. I am shocked with what skills I have acquired and what I am now able to do with certain software.
